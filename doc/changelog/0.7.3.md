## 0.7.3

* Added to `object Gen`:
  * `uuid` for UUIDs.
  * `randomSeed` to generate and apply a random (non-determinstic) seed.
  * `setOptionalSeed`.

* Added to `Gen` instances:
  * `>>` to discard the result and `flatMap` to something else.
  * `withSeed`.
  * `withOptionalSeed`.
  * `withRandomSeed`.
